Lab 1: Three Nouns

In lab one, we are supposed to use the Random Lists Random Noun Generator to find 3 random nouns and make a website based on them. My random nouns were:

  1. Pets
  2. Wash
  3. Recess

The way that I will use these three words together will be for washing pets during recess. I'm not the most creative so I hope this much will suffice.

Pets

Dogs may just be one of (if not, the) best animal companions you could ask for. However, in their abundant playfullness, they tend to get super dirty.

Wash

Whether you put them in a tub yourself or send them to the groomers, you still have to get them washed up. It takes a lot of time, energy, and sometimes money!

Recess

Guess who else has a ton of extra energy? That's right, kids! Not to mention, just like dogs, kids love to play (they have a specific time for it every day). That includes playing with water... and suds.

And so...

You can probably guess where this is going. I propose a new way of making sure your pooch gets clean, free of cost and mostly morally gray. Kids would just love to spend some time with your dog. So what if we were to let kids get their energy out by washing our dogs during recess time- free of cost while also supplying a fun activity to children? I, for one, think it's a great idea.
